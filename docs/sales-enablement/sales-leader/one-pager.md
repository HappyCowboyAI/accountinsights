# Account Insights

**Know which accounts your team should prioritize — before they miss the ones that matter.**

---

## The Problem

Your reps know their top 5 accounts. But the other 95% of their book is running on autopilot. Right now across your team:

- **High-value accounts are being neglected** — no meetings in 30 days, engagement dropping, but nobody's flagged it because the rep is focused on new business
- **Reps are chasing the wrong prospects** — spreading time across 200 accounts equally instead of focusing on the 20 that match your best-customer behavioral profile
- **Expansion is hiding in plain sight** — customer accounts with 3x the engagement of similar-sized accounts, but no open pipeline because nobody compared the data
- **Relationships are single-threaded** — the account looks healthy because the engagement score is fine, but it's one champion carrying the entire relationship

You find out when the customer churns or the competitor wins. The data knew weeks ago.

---

## What Account Insights Does

Monitors every account in your book — not just the ones with active deals — and surfaces the five patterns that humans miss:

| Insight | What Your Team Misses | What You Get |
|---------|----------------------|--------------|
| **ICP Fit** | Reps treating all prospect accounts the same | Behavioral scoring showing which accounts match your closed-won profile |
| **Relationship Health** | Single-threaded risk hiding behind good engagement scores | Multi-threading depth and exec coverage across every account |
| **Engagement Momentum** | Accounts heating up or cooling down right now | Surge detection (new exec meetings, email spikes) and decline alerts |
| **Whitespace & Expansion** | Customer accounts outpacing their current footprint | Accounts with engagement 3x above benchmark but no expansion pipeline |
| **Account Neglect** | High-value accounts your team hasn't touched | Coverage gaps on accounts worth $500K+ with zero recent activity |
