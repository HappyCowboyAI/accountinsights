# Sales Enablement — Sales Leader Persona

**Audience:** VPs of Sales, CROs, Sales Managers, Regional Sales Directors

**When to use:** When your buyer or champion owns a number and manages a team. They care about account prioritization, relationship visibility, expansion revenue, and making sure high-value accounts don't slip through the cracks.

---

## Key Messaging Themes

- **Account prioritization** — stop guessing which accounts to chase and start scoring them against your best-customer behavioral profile
- **Relationship visibility** — see which accounts have deep, multi-threaded relationships and which are single-threaded risks before a deal is at stake
- **Expansion detection** — identify customer accounts with 3x the engagement of similar-sized accounts because they're telling you they want more
- **Coverage accountability** — know which high-value accounts your team hasn't touched in 30 days, no more invisible neglect

## Sales Leader vs. RevOps Positioning

Sales leaders evaluate on **outcomes and team leverage**. They ask:
- "Will this help me hit my number?"
- "How do I know my team is covering the right accounts?"
- "Where are the expansion opportunities we're leaving on the table?"

Lead with specific account scenarios, coverage gaps, and revenue impact. Use concrete examples — "the $2M customer account nobody's talked to in 30 days" resonates more than "account health monitoring." Frame everything as a tool that makes them a better leader and their team more focused, not a surveillance system.
