# Sales Enablement — RevOps Persona

**Audience:** Revenue Operations leaders, Sales Operations managers, CRM administrators

**When to use:** When your buyer or champion sits in RevOps and cares about account hygiene, scoring transparency, data quality, and scalable coverage reporting.

---

## Key Messaging Themes

- **Account hygiene at scale** — surface accounts with zero engagement, single-threaded relationships, and coverage gaps automatically
- **Scoring transparency** — every score is built from OOTB activity metrics with fully visible calculations
- **Operational efficiency** — no implementation burden, deploys via bookmarklets on existing People.ai data
- **Metric-driven process** — 12 FormulaMetrics computing transparent ratios and scores, no black-box AI

## RevOps vs. Sales Leader Positioning

RevOps buyers evaluate on **process and scalability**. They ask:
- "How do the metrics actually work under the hood?"
- "What's the implementation and maintenance burden?"
- "How do we measure coverage and accountability at scale?"

Lead with scoring transparency, account hygiene automation, and zero-admin deployment. Avoid overly emotional account stories — RevOps wants systematic visibility into how scores are computed, what data powers them, and how they scale across every team without manual overhead.
