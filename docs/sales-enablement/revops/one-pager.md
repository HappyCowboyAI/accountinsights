# Account Insights

**Turn People.ai activity data into account-level intelligence your team acts on.**

---

## The Problem

Your team captures thousands of sales activities every week. Meetings, emails, calls — all flowing into People.ai. But between pipeline reviews, critical account-level patterns go unnoticed:

- **Accounts going dark** — engagement declining weeks before anyone flags it
- **Single-threaded relationships** — one contact leaves and the entire account relationship collapses
- **Missing exec coverage** — mid-level meetings happening but no executive engagement in weeks
- **Expansion signals hiding** — accounts with disproportionate activity but no upsell in motion

These patterns exist in your data today. Nobody's reading them at the account level.

---

## What Account Insights Does

Account Insights analyzes your existing People.ai engagement metrics — no new data sources, no new integrations — and surfaces five categories of actionable account intelligence:

| Insight | What It Catches | When It Matters |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|
| **ICP Fit Profiling** | Accounts matching best-customer engagement patterns | Prioritizing outreach and resource allocation |
| **Relationship Health** | Single-threading, exec gaps, one-directional email | Before a key contact leaves or goes dark |
| **Engagement Momentum** | 7d vs 30d activity acceleration/deceleration | 2-3 weeks before accounts lose momentum visibly |
| **Whitespace & Expansion** | High engagement + small footprint, new stakeholders | Before competitors capture the expansion opportunity |
| **Account Neglect** | Zero meetings, zero emails, declining engagement | When high-value accounts are slipping through the cracks |

---

## How It Works

**Built on the data you already have.** Every signal uses People.ai's out-of-the-box metrics — engagement levels, meetings held, emails exchanged, people engaged, executives engaged, email responsiveness. No custom fields. No SalesAI dependency for the base product.

**12 FormulaMetrics with transparent calculations.** Stakeholder breadth ratio, exec coverage percentage, engagement momentum score, email responsiveness rate, meeting acceleration — all computed from OOTB data. You can see exactly why an account scored the way it did.

**Composite scoring across 5 categories.** Each account gets a grade across ICP Fit, Relationship Health, Engagement Momentum, Whitespace potential, and Neglect risk. No black-box AI — every score traces back to specific activity metrics.

**Three delivery surfaces:**

1. **EDB Tables** — 5 category-specific views plus a composite summary table in People.ai. Each insight type has a dedicated table with the exact columns that matter. Deploy in minutes.

2. **SalesAI Signals** — AI-generated insight cards that appear directly on the account record. Reps see the most critical pattern with a specific recommended action.

3. **Slack Digest** — On-demand account intelligence. Any rep types `/account-insights` and gets a full analysis posted to their channel — neglected accounts, surging accounts, expansion candidates — in under 3 minutes.
