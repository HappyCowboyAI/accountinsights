Options Deployment Approaches

Account Insights offers two ways to deploy SalesAI signals. The unified approach is recommended for most customers as it provides intelligent prioritization and lower signal volume.

Recommended
Unified Signal
One "Account Intelligence" signal per account. The LLM evaluates all 6 patterns (Neglect, Declining, Relationship, Whitespace, ICP Fit, Surging) and surfaces only the highest-priority insight.
  • Lower signal volume — one insight per account
  • Intelligent prioritization across categories
  • Simpler deployment — one signal config
  • Updates 2-3 times weekly
Alternative
Individual Signals
5 separate signals, one per insight category. Each fires independently when its threshold is met. More granular but higher total signal volume.
  • Granular — multiple signals can fire per account
  • Category-specific thresholds and prompts
  • More flexibility for custom workflows
  • Higher signal volume (up to 5x per account)
  • No cross-category prioritization
  • 5 separate configs to manage
Hierarchy Priority Hierarchy

When the unified signal evaluates an account, it scores all patterns and displays only the highest-priority one that exceeds its threshold. This ensures the most urgent insight always surfaces first.

Signals Signal Categories

Each category evaluates a specific dimension of account health. The unified signal scores all 6 patterns (the 5 categories below plus "Engagement Surging" as a positive momentum indicator) and presents the most critical one.

🚨
Account Neglect
Priority 1 — Most Urgent
What It Detects
High-value accounts being ignored — customers or accounts with pipeline that have gone dark.
Confidence Threshold
Score > 0.70
Trigger Conditions
  • Meetings (30d) = 0 or 1
  • Emails received (30d) = 0
  • Engagement level < 20
  • Account type = Customer or has open pipeline
  • High annual revenue with near-zero activity
Action: Re-engage within 48 hours. Any row in this category with Account Type = "Customer" is urgent. Open Opps > 0 with zero activity = deal at risk from neglect.
📉
Engagement Declining
Priority 2
What It Detects
Accounts where activity is decelerating — cooling down before they go fully dark.
Confidence Threshold
Score > 0.60
Trigger Conditions
  • Meeting count 7d vs 30d ratio < 0.5 (normalized to 4.3x)
  • Email received 7d vs 30d ratio < 0.5
  • Engagement level below 40 and falling
  • People engaged dropping
  • Previously active account going quiet
Action: Act within 1 week to reverse the trend. Schedule a meeting, send a personalized outreach, or escalate internally.
🤝
Relationship Health (Poor)
Priority 3
What It Detects
Under-invested relationships — single-threaded accounts, no exec access, one-way communication.
Confidence Threshold
Score < 0.40 (inverted — low score = poor health)
Trigger Conditions
  • People engaged < 3 (single-threading risk)
  • Execs engaged = 0 (no executive access)
  • Email responsiveness < 0.2 (one-way communication)
  • Emails sent >> emails received (chasing)
  • No meetings in last 7 days despite active pipeline
Action: Broaden within 1 week. Map additional stakeholders, request exec introductions, and diversify communication channels.
💎
Whitespace & Expansion
Priority 4
What It Detects
Untapped expansion potential in existing accounts — high engagement with small deal footprint.
Confidence Threshold
Score > 0.65
Trigger Conditions
  • Engagement level > 60 on customer account
  • New people engaged appearing (expanding contacts)
  • Exec activities high relative to current deal footprint
  • Activity intensity high relative to annual revenue
  • Customer account with few/no open opportunities
Action: Reposition within 2 weeks. Explore upsell/cross-sell, engage new stakeholders, and open expansion conversations.
🎯
ICP Fit
Priority 5
What It Detects
Accounts whose behavioral engagement pattern matches your best customers — strong fit for prioritized outreach.
Confidence Threshold
Score > 0.70 (prospect accounts)
Trigger Conditions
  • Engagement level > 60
  • Exec coverage > 25% of activities with Dir/VP/Exec
  • Email responsiveness > 0.4 (two-way conversation)
  • People engaged > 5 per month (stakeholder breadth)
  • Meeting frequency > 4/month
  • Pattern matches closed-won account behavioral profile
Action: Prioritize outreach. This account is behaving like your best customers — move it up the priority queue.
Reference OOTB Metrics Used by Signals

All signals analyze these People.ai out-of-the-box account metrics. No custom fields or org-specific data required. All slugs prefixed with ootb_account_.
